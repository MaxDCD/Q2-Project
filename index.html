✨The Philippines: Places You Must Travel To ✨


The Philippines, an archipelago consisting of 7,000+ islands filled with beautiful and breathtaking scenery is a must to travel to.

Here's 3 places to check out that you should definitely add to your bucket list!



Bohol

  • Famous for being the home of The Chocolate Hills (picture)
  • Tarsier Sanctuary where endangered tarsiers reside
  • Many ancestral homes and old churches that date back to the Spanish colonization


Palawan

  • Rated as the number one tourist spot in the Philippines, it's no wonder that Palawan is added to this list!
  • Two of the most visited spots are the infamous Puerto Princessa Subterranean River and El Nido's lagoon (picture)
  • Many diving spots with unique surroundings filled with cool sea creatures (sea turtles, manta rays, seahorses, etc.)

Boracay

  • Famous for its beaches filled with white sand and surrounded by its clear, mesmerizing, and azure water (picture)
  • One of the top destinations for relaxation
  • You can find many resorts, restaurants, and tons of cool activities to try (e.g. banana boat rides, parasailing, scuba diving, island hopping).
